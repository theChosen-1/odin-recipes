Pizza:

Description:

Pakistani-style chicken pizza is a flavorful fusion of traditional Italian pizza and bold South Asian spices. The base is a soft, hand-tossed crust slathered with a spicy tomato sauce infused with garlic, chili flakes, and desi masalas. The topping features tender pieces of marinated chicken—often cooked in tikka or tandoori style—adding a smoky, aromatic punch. Layers of mozzarella cheese melt over the chicken, creating a rich, savory contrast to the heat.

To enhance the desi twist, the pizza is often garnished with sliced onions, green chilies, and fresh coriander. Some versions even drizzle a touch of mint chutney or garlic mayo over the top for extra zest. Baked until golden and bubbling, this Pakistani-style chicken pizza is a perfect blend of fiery spice and cheesy indulgence, reflecting the country's love for bold, hearty flavors.

Ingridients:

  • Boneless chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • All-purpose flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Warm water
  • Tomato puree
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Red chili flakes
  • Oregano
  • Garlic powder or minced garlic
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh coriander (cilantro)
  • Black olives (optional)
  • Mint chutney (optional)
  • Garlic mayo (optional)

Steps:

  1. Marinate Chicken - Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Let it marinate for 30 minutes or more.
  2. Cook Chicken - Sauté the marinated chicken in a pan until fully cooked and slightly browned.
  3. Prepare Dough - Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, oil, and warm water. Knead into a soft dough and let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  4. Make Sauce - Combine tomato puree, ketchup, garlic, chili flakes, oregano, and salt. Cook for a few minutes until thickened.
  5. Assemble Pizza - Roll out the dough, spread the sauce, add cooked chicken, vegetables, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake - Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish and Serve - Top with fresh coriander, green chilies, or optional chutneys. Slice and enjoy!
