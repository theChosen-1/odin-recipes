Fettuccini Pasta:

Description:

Fettuccine pasta is a classic Italian noodle known for its flat, thick shape, perfect for holding rich, creamy sauces. Made from egg and flour, fettuccine is traditionally paired with indulgent sauces like Alfredo or carbonara, where the wide strands soak up every bit of flavor. Its hearty texture makes it an ideal base for both vegetarian and meat-based dishes, offering a comforting and satisfying meal.

Whether served with a simple garlic and olive oil drizzle or a full-bodied cream sauce with chicken, mushrooms, or seafood, fettuccine provides a versatile foundation. Popular in both home kitchens and upscale restaurants, it captures the essence of Italian comfort food with a touch of elegance.

Ingridients:

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Boneless chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter or oil
  • Onion
  • Capsicum (bell pepper)
  • Green chilies (optional)
  • Fresh cream or heavy cream
  • Milk
  • Cheese (cheddar or mozzarella, optional)
  • Oregano or mixed Italian herbs
  • Fresh coriander or parsley (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Marinate Chicken - Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook Pasta - Boil fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook Chicken - Saut√© marinated chicken in butter or oil until fully cooked and lightly browned.
  4. Prepare Sauce - In the same pan, cook onions, capsicum, and green chilies. Add cream, a splash of milk, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer until thickened.
  5. Combine - Add cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce. Mix well and let it heat through.
  6. Garnish and Serve - Top with cheese, fresh coriander or parsley, and serve hot.
